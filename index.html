The purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the viewer, and the pleasent exercise of effort and ingenuity for the grower

By contrast with other plant cultivation prctices, bonsai is not intended for production of food or for medicine. Instead, bonsai practice focuses on long term cultivation and shaping of one or more small trees growing in a container
